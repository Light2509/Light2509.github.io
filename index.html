What is School Climate?

According to the National Center on Safe Supportive Learning Environments, school climate describes the school conditions that influence student learning. It is a multidimensional concept that includes:

  • Safety: Physical and emotional security for all students and staff.
  • Teaching & Learning: Quality of instruction and academic expectations.
  • Relationships: Connectedness between students, teachers, and families.
  • Environment: The physical plant and disciplinary structures.

Core Domains of School Climate

Engagement

Strong relationships between students, teachers, families, and schools.

  • Cultural Competence
  • School Participation
  • Respect for Diversity

Safety

Protection from violence, bullying, harassment, and substance use.

  • Emotional Safety
  • Physical Safety
  • Bullying Prevention

Environment

Appropriate facilities, well-managed classrooms, and fair disciplinary policies.

  • Physical Plant
  • Academic Support
  • Mental Health Resources
